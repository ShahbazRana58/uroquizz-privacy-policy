1. Introduction

UroQuizz (“we”, “our”, “the app”) is an educational application for medical students. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our app, including through Google or Apple Sign-In and subscription features.

By creating an account or using the app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you use UroQuizz, we may collect the following information:

Account Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password
  • Year of training

Usage and Learning Data

  • Quiz answers and scores
  • Progress tracking
  • Learning history

Payments

  • Subscription status (active / expired) via Stripe
  • Payment details are processed directly by Stripe; we do not store your card information.

Sign-In Providers

  • Google Sign-In
  • Apple Sign-In

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the app experience
  • Track learning progress and quiz performance
  • Manage subscriptions and payment verification
  • Communicate important app updates and notifications (via email if needed)
  • Ensure app security and prevent abuse

4. Third-Party Service Providers

To operate and improve UroQuizz, we use trusted third-party service providers that help us deliver core app functionality. These providers may process personal data only on our behalf and under strict data protection agreements. They are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

These services include providers that support:

  • Secure cloud hosting and database infrastructure
  • Email delivery for account-related communications (such as verification and important updates)

Payment processing for subscriptions is handled securely by Stripe. We do not store or have access to your card or payment details.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored on secure cloud infrastructure protected by industry-standard security measures. We take appropriate technical and organizational steps to safeguard personal data, including:

  • Encrypted transmission of sensitive information
  • Restricted and authenticated access to systems
  • Ongoing monitoring to prevent unauthorized access

When you delete your account, your personal data is removed from our active systems. To use the app again, you will need to create a new account.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and view your account information
  • Edit your profile (except your training year)
  • Delete your account, which will remove all personal data

To exercise any of these rights, use the account settings in the app or contact us at support@uroquizz.com.

7. Children’s Privacy

UroQuizz is designed for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us to request deletion.
